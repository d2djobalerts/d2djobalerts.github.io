President Droupadi Murmu has appointed new governors for nine states in India.

Updated on : 29 july,2024 an hours ago

President Droupadi Murmu has made several new appointments. Here are the new appointments with their previous designations :

Responsibilities

  • Haribhau Kisanrao Bagde (Former Maharashtra Assembly Speaker) is the new Governor of Rajasthan.
  • Jishnu Dev Varma (Former Tripura Deputy Chief Minister) will be the Governor of Telangana.
  • Om Prakash Mathur (Former Rajya Sabha MP) is the Governor of Sikkim.
  • Santosh Kumar Gangwar (Former Union Minister) will be the Governor of Jharkhand.
  • Ramen Deka (Former Lok Sabha MP from Assam) is the Governor of Chhattisgarh.
  • CH Vijayashankar (Former Karnataka Minister) is appointed as Governor of Meghalaya.
  • CP Radhakrishnan (Current Governor of Jharkhand) will now be the Governor of Maharashtra.
  • Gulab Chand Kataria (Current Governor of Assam) is the new Governor of Punjab and Administrator of Chandigarh.
  • Lakshman Prasad Acharya (Current Governor of Sikkim) will be the Governor of Assam and has been given additional charge as Governor of Manipur.
  • K Kailashnathan (Former IAS officer) is the new Lieutenant Governor of Puducherry.
