ServiceNow is looking for Software Quality Engineer with bachelors degree as qualification. The complete information like qualification, skills, etc are provided below.

Job Description

Company Name ServiceNow
Role Software Quality Engineer
Location Hyderabad / India
Experience Freshers/Experienced
Qualification Bachelor's degree in Computer Science

Responsibilities

  • Design, develop, and execute test plans, test cases, and test scripts to thoroughly test software applications for functionality, performance, reliability, and scalability.
  • Drive QA processes, standards, and methodologies to ensure consistent and high-quality deliverables.
  • Collaborate closely with cross-functional teams, including software developers, product managers, and project managers, to identify and resolve quality issues in a timely manner.
  • Monitor and analyze test results, identify trends, and provide recommendations for continuous improvement.
  • Stay updated on industry trends, emerging technologies, and best practices in QA/testing methodologies to drive innovation and improvement.

Skills & Eligibility Criteria

  • Bachelor's degree in Computer Science, Engineering, or related field.
  • Effective communication and interpersonal skills, with the ability to collaborate with cross-functional teams.
  • Ability to work independently and manage multiple priorities in a fast-paced environment.
  • Excellent analytical and problem-solving skills, with a keen attention to detail.
  • Proven leadership abilities, with a track record of successfully leading and mentoring QA teams.
  • Strong knowledge of software testing methodologies, tools, and techniques
