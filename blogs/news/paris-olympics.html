India At Paris Olympics 2024 Today

Updated on : 03 August 2024

India at Paris Olympics Manu Bhaker,

who had already won two medals, almost won a third at the Paris Olympics but ended up fourth in the women's 25m pistol event on August 3, 2024. She finishes her Olympics as the first athlete from Independent India to win two medals at the same Games.

Bhaker qualified in second place in the preliminary round yesterday, while another Indian, Esha Singh, did not make the cut.


Today, Indian athletes will compete in golf, skeet shooting, archery, sailing, and boxing. Deepika Kumari is a key player to watch as she competes in the round of 16 in women’s archery singles. If she wins, the quarterfinals, semifinals, and final will all take place today.

Day 7 was a good day for Indian athletes: Lakshya Sen reached the semifinals in men’s badminton singles, Manu Bhaker did well in the 25m pistol qualification, and the Indian hockey team defeated Australia.
